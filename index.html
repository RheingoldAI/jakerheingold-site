Thoughts, Opinions, Experiences

I really feel there is a massive gap between the everyday casual AI user and that next tier of vibe coding and building apps. I think these everyday users have the appetite to up level their AI use in the ever-changing tech world, but are cautious and intimidated. Viral posts people see read like: "Build an AI bot that serves as your Chief of Staff in 10 minutes"; "I spent 2 hours coding the next big marketing app. Traditional marketing is dead."

I want to demystify AI for the intimidated consumer. I'm going to #BuildInPublic and document what's working for me, what isn't, my successes, failures, ask for help, and hopefully build a community along the way.
